# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

VhyxSeal is an open, welcoming project. Everyone participating in the
community — through issues, pull requests, discussions, or any other
channel — is expected to follow these standards.

**Expected behaviour:**

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Give and accept feedback graciously
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users
- Assume good intent; ask for clarification before escalating

**Unacceptable behaviour:**

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks of any kind
- Discrimination based on age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender
  identity, nationality, race, religion, sexual orientation, or experience level
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, report it to:
**conduct@vhyxara.com** (placeholder — update before public launch)

Reports are handled confidentially. We will review every report and
respond within 7 days.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in a warning, temporary ban, or permanent removal
from the project, at the discretion of the maintainers, depending on
the severity and pattern of behaviour.

Project maintainers who do not enforce this Code of Conduct in good
faith may face consequences as determined by project leadership.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and in public
spaces when an individual is representing the VhyxSeal project.
